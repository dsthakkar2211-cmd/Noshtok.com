Operational Model

01 02 03 04 05 06 07 08 09 10 11
STEP 1

Noshtok Account Creation

Prospective women entrepreneurs will register as culinary partners on the Noshtok platform through a guided, user-friendly process.

STEP 2

Kitchen Certification

Home kitchens will undergo a basic hygiene and safety assessment, which can be a combination of virtual checks and adherence to self-certification guidelines, to ensure compliance with the Balckly 3.1 protocol.

STEP 3

Live Feed Integration

A simple 4K camera system will be installed to enable the Live Kitchen Feed, seamlessly integrated with the Noshtok platform for real-time streaming.

STEP 4

Menu Development Support

Assistance will be provided in crafting optimized menus, developing effective pricing strategies, and writing appealing dish descriptions to attract customers.

STEP 5

Comprehensive Training

Entrepreneurs will receive thorough training on utilizing Noshtok's POS, inventory management, and reporting features to ensure smooth operations.

STEP 6

Order Placement

Customers will place orders via the Noshtok app or website, with options for customization facilitated by the "Chat with Chef" feature.

STEP 7

Live Preparation

Meals will be prepared in the home kitchen, with the process visible to customers through the Live Kitchen Feed, reinforcing transparency.

STEP 8

Packaging & Delivery

Standardized packaging guidelines will be provided, and integration with local delivery partners or self-delivery options will be established to ensure efficient and safe delivery.

STEP 9

Customer Reviews

Noshtok's integrated review system, including transparency ratings, will encourage and maintain high standards of quality and service.

STEP 10

Performance Analytics

Entrepreneurs will utilize Noshtok's reporting features to track sales, identify best-selling items, and manage inventory efficiently, enabling data-driven growth.

STEP 11

Community Support

A dedicated network for women culinary entrepreneurs will be established to facilitate sharing of best practices, collaboration, and ongoing peer support.
